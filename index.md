Privacy Policy for JobTracker

Effective Date: 2026-02-09

JobTracker is a simple job application tracking app designed to help users organize their job search.

Information Collection and Use

JobTracker does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All job-related information entered into the app (such as job titles, company names, dates, and notes) is stored locally on the user’s device.

Data Sharing

JobTracker does not share user data with third parties.

Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on the device, users have full control over their information. Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

Third-Party Services

JobTracker does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children’s Privacy

JobTracker is not specifically designed for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
